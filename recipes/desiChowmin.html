Desi Chowmein

What is it?

Chowmein is a popular Asian dish where stir fried noodles are tossed with lots of vegetables and sauces.
This recipe is of the Indian street style veg chowmein noodles which is an integral part of the Indo Chinese cuisine.

What you need?

For Chowmein

  • 1 Packet noodles, boiled and strained
  • 4 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Capsicum
  • Spring Onion

For Sauce

  • 2 tbsp Soya Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp tomato Sauce
  • Chilli Sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp Black Pepper powder

How to make?

  1. To prepare sauce
    2.
    1. In a bowl, add all sauce ingredients. You can crumble Chicken Stock Cube to the sauce mixture.
    2. Keep aside.

  2. To prepare chowmein
    3.
    1. Add oil in wok.
    2. Add onions and stir fry for aminute.
    3. Add all vegetables except spring onion and stir fry on high flame for a minute.
    4. Add spring onion in the last 30 secand stir well.
    5. Remove stir fried vegetables and keep it aside.
    6. Pour remaining oil in thewok and heat.
    7. Add chopped ginger and garlic and saute for about a minute unit light brown.
    8. Add the sauce mixture
    9. Let cook for 2 mins, don't let the sauce dry up
    10. Turn flame high and add noodles.
    11. Give the noodles a good mix until well coated with sauce.
    12. Add stir fried vegetables to noodles and stir fry on high flame.

  3. Serve
