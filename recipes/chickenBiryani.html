Chicken Biryani

What is it?

Biryani is made with marinated meat like chicken, mutton, fragrant basmati rice, whole spices, coriander leaves, mint leaves, fried onions, fried nuts and raisins and lots of ghee.

Chicken biryani is the popular variety of biryani which is made with tender pieces of chicken marinated with yogurt, spices, fresh garam masala, coriander, mint and lemon juice

What you need?

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Green Chilli
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Whole Spices
    • Cinnamon
    • Clove
    • Fennel Seeds
    • Cumin Seeds
    • Bay Leaf
    • Star Anise
    • Cardamom
    • Black Pepper
  • Spice Powders
    • Chilli Powder
    • Turmeric Powder
    • Coriander Powder
    • Garam Masala
  • Curd
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Ghee or Oil

How to make?

  1. Marinating Chicken
    2.

    Take 500 grams of cleaned chicken in a bowl. I am using skinless and bone-in chicken. Make sure you cut the chicken into medium pieces which cooks fast and stays moist. To the cleaned chicken, add in curd, salt, chilli powder, garam masala powder and ginger garlic paste. Mix well and set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Soaking Basmati Rice
    3.

    While the chicken is marinating. Wash 2 cups of basmati rice multiple times till water runs clear. Cover with fresh cold water and let it soak for 30 minutes.

  3. Making Fresh Biryani Masala
    4.

    Take fennel seeds, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, star anise in a blender and powder till coarse. This is your fresh masala for biryani.

  4. Making Biryani Base
    5.

    Heat coconut oil and ghee in a kadai. Add in sliced onions and slit green chillies. Saute them till light golden in colo

  5. Further Procedure
    6.
    1. Add in freshly ground ginger and garlic paste. Saute the ginger and garlic till raw smell leaves. It will take around 2 minutes.
    2. Add in chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder and fresh garam masala powder.
    3. Saute the spice powders with onion for few seconds.

  6. Cooking Chicken
    7.
    1. Once masala is cooked, add in the marinated chicken.
    2. Saute the marinated chicken in the masala for at least 6 to 8 minutes. The chicken will be half cooked at this point.
    3. Take this chicken masala in a pressure cooker.

  7. Roasting Rice
    8.
    1. In the same pan in which you made the chicken masala. Add a tbsp of ghee.
    2. Add in the 2 cups of soaked strained rice. Roast the rice for few minutes till it turns toasty and light golden in colour.
    3. Add in the roasted rice over the chicken masala which is kept in the pressure cooker.

  8. Cooking the Chicken Biryani
    9.
    1. Add in chopped coriander and mint leaves
    2. Pour in 1 cup of coconut milk. This is optional. You can use water instead of coconut milk.
    3. Pour in 2 cups of water, and add salt to taste.
    4. Bring this to a full boil. Once it starts boiling give it one good stir. cover the lid of the pressure cooker.
    5. Pressure cook for 1 whistle, reduce the flame to lowest possible and cook for 5 minutes. Switch off the flame and let the pressure release by itself.
    6. Open the cooker and mix gently using a spatula or fork.

  9. Serve!
